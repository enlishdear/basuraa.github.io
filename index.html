My projects:

Competing in the WRO:

Between 2015 and 2018, I participated in various robotics competitions (such as the World Robot Olympiad", or WRO), both at a regional and national level, in which I acquired leadership skills, as well as programming and mechanical skills. Furthermore, I also improved my social capabilities, working with a team of individuals towards a common goal.

Creation and maintenance of a webpage:

For the whole school year of 2019, I updated and maintained an educational webpage for my classmates in 3rd ESO, with the purpose of providing new study methods for the upcoming exams. This, in turn, improved my design skills, since I had to design a webpage, as well as all of its content.

Translation of a webpage:

Between May and August of 2020, I translated the webpage of the program RiiConnect24 to Catalan, so that people who didn't understand English could use it without any kind of issues. This also helped me improve my translation skills, thereby improving my English level considerably.

Icon design for an application:

During 2021, I designed icons for the popular Wii and GameCube emulator Dolphin. This allowed me to improve my knowledge in design and participate in a large colaborative effort, with the objective of offering a much better experience with the application and its functions.

Participation in the math club:

Between 2017 and 2020, I participated actively in Mas Camarena's math club, hosted by math teacher Luisa Cuadrado. In it, I developed a great interest in mathematics and improved my knowledge in this subject, all the while improving my grades. In addition, thanks to the club, I also participated in a wide array of math competitions, such as "Estalmat" o "Cangur", both of which helped me improve my logical thinking.
